# Privacy Policy — CGM Evaluator

**Last updated:** June 2026
**Developer:** AAPS Assist / Antonio Wanderley

---

## 1. Overview

CGM Evaluator ("the App") is a free, offline decision-support tool for people with diabetes who use continuous glucose monitoring (CGM) sensors. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices.

**In short: the App collects no personal data, sends nothing to any server, and requires no account.**

---

## 2. Data Collection

The App does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

Specifically:
- No name, email, or account is required.
- No health or medical data is transmitted to any server.
- No analytics, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs are included.
- No internet permission is requested.

---

## 3. Local Storage

All data you enter (glucose readings, evaluation history) is saved **locally on your device only**, using Android's SharedPreferences storage. This data:
- Never leaves your device.
- Can be cleared at any time from within the App (Sensor Log → Clear History).
- Is deleted when you uninstall the App.

---

## 4. Permissions

The App requests **no Android permissions**. It does not access your camera, location, contacts, microphone, storage, or network.

---

## 5. Third Parties

The App does not integrate with any third-party service, analytics platform, or advertising network.

---

## 6. Children

The App does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

---

## 7. Medical Disclaimer

CGM Evaluator is a decision-support tool for informational purposes only. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional before making changes to your diabetes management.
